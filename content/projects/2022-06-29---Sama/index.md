The app is made for mobile apps and tablets. It can be used by the student to watch lessons, view the calendar, use interactive materials and download documents to use at the in person classes. Also, at the same app, teachers can view the calendar aswell, submit attendances and documents.

Along with it, principals and coordinator can access the admin panel to create and manage teams, users and teachers throughout the years.
